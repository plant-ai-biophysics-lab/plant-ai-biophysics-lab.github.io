**Summary:**

AgKit is an advanced sensing system specifically designed for precision agriculture. Its core purpose is to monitor crop performance and estimate yields by leveraging state-of-the-art AI technology. AgKit is equipped with a lightning system and high-resolution RGB cameras that can be used for day and night-time imaging. Additional sensors such as LiDAR and spectroradiometer can be integrated into the system, expanding its capabilities to derive insights such as detecting flowers and fruits at different stages, diseases, and canopy architectural traits. One of the key features of AgKit is it can be easily mounted on tractors and used during regular farm operations such as spraying, making it a cost-effective solution for growers. With GPS sensors on board, we can generate yield maps with centimeter-level accuracy. AgKit has already been deployed in various fields, including grape, almond, olive, and strawberry, and we are excited to continue providing growers with the tools and insights they need to optimize their field management.
